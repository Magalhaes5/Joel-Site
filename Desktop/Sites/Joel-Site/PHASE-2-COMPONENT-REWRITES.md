## Key Conversion Optimization Principles Applied:

### 1. Speed as Differentiator
Every section emphasizes 2-7 day delivery vs. months with agencies

### 2. Value Anchoring
Pricing compared to agency rates (€2,500-€12,000) positions packages as exceptional deals

### 3. Social Proof Throughout
- Hero: 5-star rating + 12+ businesses
- Trust Strip: "Happy Clients" + "5-Star Rated"
- Testimonials: Enhanced with specific results
- Pricing: "MOST POPULAR" badge

### 4. Outcome-Focused Copy
Every service/package now emphasizes business results:
- "Turn visitors into customers"
- "3x more bookings"
- "Commands premium pricing"
- "Drives real ROI"

### 5. Risk Reversal
30-day satisfaction guarantee removes purchase anxiety

### 6. Urgency Creation
"Reserve Your Spot" CTAs imply limited availability

### 7. Specific > Vague
- "7 days" not "fast"
- "€899" not "affordable"
- "3x bookings" not "more customers"
- "12+ businesses" not "many clients"
