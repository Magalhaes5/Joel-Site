Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with us

How much do your services typically cost?

Our fees vary based on the complexity and scope of services. Individual tax returns start at €800/year. Small business packages (bookkeeping + tax) start at €6,000/year. We offer transparent, fixed-fee pricing for most services so there are no surprises. During your discovery call, we'll provide a detailed quote tailored to your specific needs.

Do you work with startups and early-stage companies?

Absolutely! We have extensive experience with startups and high-growth companies. We offer startup-friendly pricing packages and understand the unique challenges of early-stage businesses - from entity selection to fundraising preparation to R&D tax credits. Many of our startup clients have grown into multi-million euro businesses while staying with us.

What's your typical response time?

We pride ourselves on responsiveness. Urgent matters receive same-day attention. Routine questions are typically answered within 4 business hours. Complex inquiries requiring research are acknowledged immediately with a timeline for a complete response. You'll have direct email and phone access to your dedicated partner - not just junior staff.

Do you offer fixed-fee pricing or just hourly billing?

We strongly prefer fixed-fee arrangements for recurring services (tax prep, bookkeeping, audits) because it provides clarity and predictability. You'll know exactly what you're paying upfront. For project-based advisory work, we typically provide fixed-fee proposals based on scope. Hourly billing is available for ad-hoc consulting but is rarely our first choice.

Can you help with international tax matters?

Yes, international tax is one of our core specialties. We regularly handle cross-border transactions, transfer pricing, tax treaties, expat taxation, and multi-country corporate structures. Luxembourg's position as an international business hub means many of our clients have complex international tax needs, and we have the expertise to navigate them.

What accounting software do you recommend and support?

We're platform-agnostic but most commonly work with Xero, QuickBooks Online, and Sage for small to mid-sized businesses. For larger clients, we work with SAP and NetSuite. We can help you select the right system for your needs, handle the implementation, and provide training. We also integrate with modern tools like Expensify, Bill.com, and Gusto.

We'll discuss your needs, answer your questions, and determine if we're a good fit. No pressure, no obligations - just an honest conversation about how we can help your business succeed.
