Meet Our Teachers

Emma Rodriguez

RYT 500, E-RYT 200

Trained in Rishikesh and Bali, Emma brings dynamic energy to her classes. 12 years of experience blending strength, flow, and mindfulness.

Specialties: Vinyasa Flow, Power Yoga

Sophie Martin

RYT 200, Prenatal Certified

Luxembourg native specializing in prenatal and gentle Hatha. Creates welcoming, compassionate space for all bodies and experience levels.

Specialties: Hatha Yoga, Prenatal

Marco Silva

RYT 500, YACEP

15 years of Ashtanga and Power Yoga from Brazil. Known for challenging sequences and legendary arm balance workshops.

Specialties: Power Yoga, Ashtanga

Lisa Anderson

RYT 200, Yin Yoga Certified

Guides students into deep tissue release and meditative states. Her calming presence creates space for profound inner transformation.

Specialties: Yin Yoga, Restorative
