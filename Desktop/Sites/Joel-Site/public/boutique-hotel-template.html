Welcome Home

Step into Le Refuge and feel the warmth embrace you. In the heart of Luxembourg City's charming historic quarter, our boutique hotel welcomes you with open arms and genuine smiles.

We believe luxury isn't just about beautiful spaces—it's about making you feel truly at home. From the moment you arrive, our family-run hotel envelops you in comfort, care, and the kind of hospitality that comes from the heart.

With just twelve beautifully bright rooms filled with natural light and thoughtful touches, Le Refuge is your personal sanctuary in the city—a place where memories are made, connections are cherished, and every stay feels like coming home.

Our Story

Founded in 1989 by the Laurent family, Le Refuge began as a vision to create a home away from home for travelers seeking authentic Luxembourg hospitality. What started as a modest guesthouse has evolved into one of the city's most cherished boutique hotels.

In 2018, we completed an extensive renovation that preserved our historical charm while introducing contemporary luxury. Every architectural detail, from the exposed stone walls to the hand-carved wooden beams, tells a story of Luxembourg's rich heritage.

Our Philosophy

At Le Refuge, we believe that true luxury is found in the details. It's in the warmth of a genuine smile, the perfect temperature of your room, the freshness of locally-sourced ingredients at breakfast, and the attentiveness of our staff who remember your name and preferences.

Family Tradition

Now in its second generation of family ownership, Le Refuge continues to honor the values that made it special: personalized service, attention to detail, and a commitment to creating meaningful memories for every guest. Sophie Laurent, who grew up in these halls, now leads our team with the same passion her parents instilled over three decades ago.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to sustainability and community. We source ingredients from local farms, support Luxembourg artisans, use eco-friendly amenities, and ensure our operations minimize environmental impact while maximizing your comfort.

Le Refuge Restaurant

Our award-winning restaurant celebrates the finest of European cuisine with a distinct Luxembourg influence. Every dish is a journey through local traditions, seasonal ingredients, and contemporary culinary artistry.

Dine in our elegant dining room with its vaulted ceilings and candlelit ambiance, or enjoy al fresco dining on our terrace overlooking the historic quarter during warmer months.

Meet Chef Henri Dubois

Chef Henri Dubois brings over 20 years of culinary excellence to Le Refuge. Trained in Paris under Michelin-starred mentors, Chef Henri returned to his Luxembourg roots to showcase the region's incredible produce and culinary heritage. His philosophy is simple: respect the ingredients, honor tradition, and never stop innovating.

Under his leadership, Le Refuge Restaurant has earned recognition from Luxembourg's top food critics and maintains partnerships with over 30 local farms and artisan producers.

Location & Surroundings

Historic Quarter Location

Le Refuge is ideally situated in Luxembourg City's charming historic quarter, a UNESCO World Heritage site where medieval fortifications meet elegant boulevards and world-class culture.

Our location offers the perfect blend of tranquility and accessibility. Step outside our doors to discover cobblestone streets, artisan boutiques, and authentic Luxembourg cafes, while remaining just minutes from the city's business district and major attractions.

Nearby Attractions

  • Grand Ducal Palace - 3 minutes walk
  • Bock Casemates - 5 minutes walk
  • Notre-Dame Cathedral - 4 minutes walk
  • Place d'Armes - 6 minutes walk
  • National Museum of History - 7 minutes walk
  • Chemin de la Corniche - 8 minutes walk
  • Grund District - 10 minutes walk
  • Shopping District - 5 minutes walk
  • Train Station - 15 minutes by taxi
  • Airport - 20 minutes by taxi

Transportation

Airport Transfer: We offer private airport transfer service in luxury vehicles. Advance booking recommended.
