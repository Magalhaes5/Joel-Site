The Challenge

Christelle, a skilled massage therapist in Chamonix, needed a website that embodied the calm, healing atmosphere of her practice. She wanted to showcase her specialized techniques (Tui Na, acupressure, prenatal massage) while making it effortless for clients to book appointments.

Client Needs

  • Convey trust and professionalism
  • Complex service menu with 15+ treatments
  • Integrate Planity booking system
  • Stand out in competitive Chamonix market
  • Mobile-first (most clients book on phones)
  • Reflect alpine wellness aesthetic

Our Solution

  • Cinematic intro with animated logo splash
  • Full-screen video background hero
  • Ambient music with toggle control
  • Service cards with transparent pricing
  • Integrated Planity booking buttons
  • Scroll-triggered GSAP animations
  • Custom Hatton serif + Montserrat fonts

The Experience

Cinematic Entry: Visitors are greeted with an animated logo that pulses and floats, setting a meditative tone. A single click transitions them into the main site with a smooth zoom effect.

Sensory Immersion: Background video of serene mountain landscapes loops continuously, while ambient spa music fades in gently. Users can control the audio with a floating button that follows them through the site.

Clear Service Presentation: Six service cards organize treatments by type (Deep Tissue, Relaxation, Specialized, Facial) with dotted-line pricing for easy scanning. Special packages and duo massages are highlighted in premium-styled cards.

Technical Innovation

✦ Custom @font-face Integration: Hatton serif typeface loaded via .otf files for distinctive branding

✦ Fixed Background Parallax: Reviews section uses CSS background-attachment: fixed for depth

✦ Optimized Video Loading: Compressed MP4 with autoplay, muted, loop, and playsinline attributes

✦ Smart Audio Control: Volume fades from 0 to 0.4 over 3 seconds to avoid jarring entry

Results & Impact

Within the first month, 87% of all bookings came through the integrated Planity system, drastically reducing Christelle's administrative workload. The average session duration of 2.8 minutes indicates high engagement—users spend time exploring services rather than bouncing.

The conversion rate of 68% (visitors who click a booking button) is exceptional for wellness sites, attributed to the trust-building design, clear pricing, and immersive experience. Reviews on Google mention the website specifically: "Found her website and knew immediately this was the right place."

Most impressively, 73% of bookings are made on mobile devices, validating the mobile-first design approach with touch-optimized buttons and simplified navigation.
